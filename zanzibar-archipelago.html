The Zanzibar Archipelago

Zanzibar (also known as Unguja) is the most mesmerizing and fascinating Island of Africa.

Wide and clean sandbanks, clear azure ocean water, a rich underwater world, hospitable friendly locals, colorful culture, island tours, communication with amazing animals, a large selection of accommodations (from cheap cozy lodges to super-luxury hotels)...

We invite you to the fantastic world of Zanzibar!

Book the tour

We propose:

• Zanzibar Beach rest in the accommodation you wish, we will choose the best one according to your preferences and budget;

• Various Zanzibar activities with a friendly local guide and a private vehicle;

• Bush to Beach programs with Zanzibar beach rest and activities in combination with Tanzanian Safari or Kilimanjaro Climbing (sequence and duration is variable);

• Domestic and international flight tickets, one-way or roundtrip.

See the most popular activities in Zanzibar below, and write us to book your tour!

Feel free to combine the activities, choose the accommodation, and ask us the special unique requests.

We are always glad to make the vacations of your dreams.

1 DAY ACTIVITY

THE WEST SIDE OF ZANZIBAR

You will have your breakfast in the hotel. At 9:00 am your guide will take you on the west side of Zanzibar Island excursion.

You will visit:

• Stone Town for historical tours, and meeting with the social life of Zanzibar people;

• Spice Farm to see Zanzibar's economy on how we use the spice in both cooking and medicine; here you will have a hearty lunch;

• The Prison island to look at the giant turtles;

• Nakupenda sand bank for sunset and swimming.

At 8:00 pm you will be free, and our guide will drive you to your accommodation.

1 DAY ACTIVITY

THE SOUTH SIDE OF ZANZIBAR

You will have your breakfast in the hotel. At 9:00 am your guide will take you on the south side of Zanzibar Island excursion.

On this day you will visit:

• Jozani Forest for red monkeys, natural woods, and mangrove trees;

• The Rock restaurant in the ocean for lunch with an amazing view;

• Mtende, Jambiani, and Paje beaches;

• Kuza Cave an ancient limestone jungle cave with crystal clear turquoise water;

• The nice place Kae Beach for sunset.

At 8:00 pm ou will be free and a guide will drive you to your accommodation.

1 DAY ACTIVITY

THE MNEMBA ISLAND

You will have your breakfast in the hotel. At 7:30 your guide will take you on the Mnemba Island trip. Here you will have an experience of:

• snorkeling;

• communication with dolphins;

• exploring different sandbanks;

• visiting the Mnarani Aquarium in Nungwi for swimming with sea turtles.

At 12:30 you will be free. Our guide will recommend good restaurants for lunch, or drive you to your accommodation, where you can have a great rest on the beach or in your room.

BUSH TO BEACH PROGRAM

Day 1: Transfer from the Kilimanjaro National Airport / Moshi / Arusha - Tarangire National Park game drive - Overnight in Karatu / Mto Wa Mbu.

Day 2: Ngorongoro Crater game drive - Maasai Boma village visit - Overnight in Serengeti National Park.

Day 3: Morning balloon safari in Serengeti NP - Full day game drive in Serengeti NP - Overnight here.

Day 7: A half-day Mnemba Island tour - Calm beach rest.
