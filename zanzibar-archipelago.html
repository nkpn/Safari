Book the tour

We can create the most suitable itineraries to fit most requests. We are specialising in affordable tour packages for Zanzibar. They are combined with safari and beach activities. Below is a sample itinerary for the Bush to beach safari or Vice versa; if at all you are interested in something different, please feel free to send us your breakdown and we are more than happy to organise accordingly.

day 1

Tarangire
National park

In the morning after breakfast you will be collected by your guide driver, and your safari will commence. Drive from Moshi or Arusha to Tarangire National park. Like it said, Tarangire is home to a multitude of migratory wildlife species which can be found in the permanent waters of the Tarangire River. Youâ€™ll enjoy the half-day game drive and have a picnic lunch in the park. In the later afternoon, you will have to drive to the campsite/lodge in Mto wa Mbu for dinner and an overnight stay.

day 2

Mto wa Mbu to
Manyara national park

On this day you will depart and begin the day with a game drive in Lake Manyara National Park. After enjoying a picnic lunch, you will continue on your game drive; in this National Park, the afternoon is the best time to view animals. Frequently seen in Lake Manyara National Park are zebras, impalas, monkeys, giraffes, buffalos, hippos, warthogs among many others. Before dark, you shall drive back to the campsite/lodge for dinner and an overnight stay.

day 3

Ngorongoro crater
to Zanzibar Island

Early in the morning; drive down into Ngorongoro Crater and enjoy a half day game drive. This is undoubtedly one of the best places to see the rare black rhino in Africa, not to mention the pride of lions and the magnificent black-mane lions. There are lots of colourful flamingos and a variety of water birds. In the crater you are also likely to see leopards, cheetahs, hyenas, many members of the antelope family, and small mammals of all sorts.

Packed lunch will be served at the picnic site inside the Crater floor. In the afternoon you will drive to Arusha airport to catch your flight straight to Zanzibar Island. On arrival at the Zanzibar airport meet our representative and drive to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

day 4

Zanzibar
beach

Spend a few days relaxing on Zanzibar beaches and if you wish you can engage yourself in some sea sports and other several activities offered by your hotel/lodge.

This spring has quite a history of its own. You will be served a picnic lunch.

day 5

Zanzibar beach
to Stone town

On this day you will take Stone town tour (Optional) en route you can do the famous spice tour guided by a very informative and knowledgeable guide. This experienced guide will sample some of the finest spices and herbs and explain their uses in cooking, Cosmetics or medicinal. You will then have a chance to test most of the tropical fruits growing on the Island depending on their season. Afterward proceed into Stone Town and check in at your hotel,spend the rest of the afternoon touring around the historical Town of Zanzibar.
