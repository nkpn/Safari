Zanzibar Archipelago

Zanzibar also known as Unguja is the most mesmerising and fascinating Island of Africa.

The best destinations on the Island are Stonetown, spice tour, safari blue tour, Nungwi, Kwendwa, east coast, (Jambiani, Paje Bwejuu and Kizimkazi). The best day trips are – Stone town tour, spice tour, safari blue tour, mnemba atoll, dolpion tour , prison island, jozani forest, sunset dhow cruise, sandbank picnic, and mangapwani slave chembers.

Book the tour

We can create the most suitable itineraries to fit most requests. We are specialising in affordable tour packages for Zanzibar. They are combined with safari and beach activities. Below is a sample itinerary for the Bush to beach safari or Vice versa; if at all you are interested in something different, please feel free to send us your breakdown and we are more than happy to organise accordingly.

day 1

Tarangire
National park

In the morning after breakfast you will be collected by your guide driver, and your safari will commence. Drive from Moshi or Arusha to Tarangire National park. Like it said, Tarangire is home to a multitude of migratory wildlife species which can be found in the permanent waters of the Tarangire River. You’ll enjoy the half-day game drive and have a picnic lunch in the park. In the later afternoon, you will have to drive to the campsite/lodge in Mto wa Mbu for dinner and an overnight stay.

day 2

Mto wa Mbu to
Manyara national park

On this day you will depart and begin the day with a game drive in Lake Manyara National Park. After enjoying a picnic lunch, you will continue on your game drive; in this National Park, the afternoon is the best time to view animals. Frequently seen in Lake Manyara National Park are zebras, impalas, monkeys, giraffes, buffalos, hippos, warthogs among many others. Before dark, you shall drive back to the campsite/lodge for dinner and an overnight stay.
