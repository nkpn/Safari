Video for Invitae —

How to properly give feedback

I produced for Invitae a video about how to properly give feedback. It is an essential part of the onboarding package for all new hires that describes company culture and values. It presents the correct and actionable feedback as a critical tool in the people analytics suite that powers talent operations at Invitae.

The heart of the operation

The challenge was to make the video engaging and memorable. Noone would stare at a talking head for almost seven minutes so I had to come up with a concept and the script that would turn an otherwise unremarkable presentation into something memorable and special. I decided to replace the speaker with as much animation as possible.
Also, I had to make sure that this is not going to be a generic cookie-cutter of stock footage. I created the script and decided to use visual metaphors and shaping animation as the most effective tools to capture the viewer’s attention.
After the script was approved, I gathered a team of illustrator, animator, motion designer, sound designer, colorist, video editor, cameraman and got to work. I managed the team and delivered the final product before the deadline.
