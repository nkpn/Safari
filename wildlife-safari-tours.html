Tanzania Safaris

Tanzania is a country so wealthy that it’d practically take years to document all the resources

Not only is a country proud to bear witness to the highest and largest freestanding mountain in the world but also to the rich and diverse wildlife concentrations, minerals and other resources available.If Africa’s tourism opportunities were to be summarized by one single country, that country would be Tanzania.

Book the tour

In the northern circuit national parks you will be able to encounter various animals, mainly some of these parks are seasonal such as Tarangire National park. Some examples of the northern circuit destination are such as Lake Manyara National park, Tarangire National park, Ngorongoro Crater, Serengeti National park, Lake Natron and Lake Eyasi(added destinations with more days).

Common activities in Tarangire are Morning and evening Game drives and Walking tours, Night Game drives (is allowed though not all accommodations offer this kind of tour), Bird watching, Tarangire balloon safaris in private reserves and concessions.

We’ve different packages and can definitely arrange the safari according to your preferences in different parks in the northern circuit. Whether it is Camping safari, mid-range lodge safari or budget safari, luxury safari and super luxury safari just feel free to let us know what you are looking for and our professional sales consultant will be at your service with a tailored safari package.
