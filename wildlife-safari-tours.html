3 Day Safari

The three-day safaris give the opportunity to make a short acquaintance with Tanzanian Parks. Suitable for low-budget tourists or persons, which have a few days for vacation in Tanzania.

  • 3 National Parks

    3 days - 3 parks - 3 game drives: Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Ngorongoro Crater

    • Day 1: Tarangire National Park

      Our driver will take you from the meeting point in Moshi/Arusha early in the morning and your safari will begin. You will drive to the Tarangire National Park, make a half-day game drive, have lunch in the park, and move to your accommodation for rest, dinner, and an overnight stay.

      Wildlife: elephants, rare antelope, wildebeest, zebras, giraffes, hartebeests, hyenas, waterbucks, warthogs, elands, and many others. Here are also a lot of different birds.

    • Day 2: Lake Manyara National Park

      After breakfast, our driver will collect you and move to Lake Manyara National Park for the safari game drive. In the evening you will return to your accommodation for rest, dinner and an overnight stay.

      Wildlife: elephant, buffalo, hippo, lion, leopard, cheetah, giraffe, and lots of plains game, and reputedly the largest baboon troupes in Africa.

    • Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater

      In the morning after breakfast, you will leave your accommodation and move to the Ngorongoro Crater for the game drive. After lunch in Crater, our driver will move you to your accommodation in Moshi/Arusha or the airport for your flight.

      Wildlife: lion, elephant, black rhinoceros, African buffalo, leopard, zebra, hippo, cheetah, giraffe, wildebeest, rhinos, hyena, gazelle, flamingo, jackal, African wild dog, serval, and others

      Crater: the biggest intact (unbroken) volcanic caldera on Earth.

    • Day 1: Tarangire National Park/ Lake Manyara National Park (for choose)

      Our driver will take you from the meeting point in Moshi/Arusha early in the morning and your safari will begin. You will drive to the Tarangire/Manyara National Park, make a half-day game drive, have lunch in the park, and move to your accommodation for rest, dinner, and an overnight stay.

    • Day 2: Ngorongoro Crater & Serengeti National Park

      Wildlife: lion, elephant, black rhinoceros, African buffalo, leopard, zebra, hippo, cheetah, giraffe, wildebeest, rhinos, hyena, gazelle, flamingo, jackal, African wild dog, serval, and othersCrater: the biggest intact (unbroken) volcanic caldera on Earth

    • Day 3: Serengeti National Park

      After early breakfast, our driver will come and collect you with your lunch boxes for your full-day safari game drive in the park. You will return to your lodge in the evening.

    • Day 4: Serengeti NP & Lake Eyasi

      Early after breakfast in your lodge, our driver will come to collect you with your lunch boxes. You will move to the morning game drive in Serengeti NP (you can cancel it if you want to sleep longer), and then, after lunch, you will start driving to Lake Eyasi to your accommodation. You will have enough time to walk, enjoy the Lake Eyasi flora and fauna, or make a short drive with your guide.

      Wildlife: flamingos, great white pelican, African spoonbill, Fischer's lovebird, gray-headed gulls, pied avocet, spurfowl, giraffes, zebras, leopards, hippopotamuses, several types of monkeys, and tropical birds.

      Lake Eyasi is the largest body of water in the Arusha region.

    • Day 5: Lake Eyasi & Hadzabe Hunting

      Early in the morning you will move with your guide to the Hadzabe tribe, meet them, and make morning hunting. Then you will have enough time to make photos and learn about their culture and ceremonies. After hearty lunch, our driver will move you to your accommodation in Moshi/Arusha or the airport for your flight.

      Hadzabe is one of the last remaining hunter-gatherer tribes on Earth.
