Tanzania Safaris

Tanzania is a country so wealthy that it’d practically take years to document all the resources

Not only is a country proud to bear witness to the highest and largest freestanding mountain in the world but also to the rich and diverse wildlife concentrations, minerals and other resources available.If Africa’s tourism opportunities were to be summarized by one single country, that country would be Tanzania.

In the northern circuit national parks you will be able to encounter various animals, mainly some of these parks are seasonal such as Tarangire National park. Some examples of the northern circuit destination are such as Lake Manyara National park, Tarangire National park, Ngorongoro Crater, Serengeti National park, Lake Natron and Lake Eyasi(added destinations with more days).

Common activities in Tarangire are Morning and evening Game drives and Walking tours, Night Game drives (is allowed though not all accommodations offer this kind of tour), Bird watching, Tarangire balloon safaris in private reserves and concessions.

We’ve different packages and can definitely arrange the safari according to your preferences in different parks in the northern circuit. Whether it is Camping safari, mid-range lodge safari or budget safari, luxury safari and super luxury safari just feel free to let us know what you are looking for and our professional sales consultant will be at your service with a tailored safari package.

Camping Safari

This particular safari involves camping at Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Ngorongoro and Serengeti.
  • 2 Days

    Camping Safari in Tarangire and Ngorongoro Crater

    • Day 1: Arrival, Airport pick up

      You will be picked by our professional driver, to the lodge for the briefing and all the necessary information about your trip, dinner and overnight stay.

    • Day 2: Moshi, Arusha to Tarangire

      Your day will begin with an early morning breakfast after your safari will commence from Moshi/Arusha heading to Tarangire national park. Like it said, Tarangire is home to a multitude of migratory wildlife species which can be found in the permanent waters of the Tarangire River. You’ll enjoy the half-day game drive and have a picnic lunch in the park. In the later afternoon, you will have to drive to the campsite in Mto wa Mbu for dinner and an overnight stay.

    • Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater to Moshi

      On this day you will have a very early morning breakfast then drive from Mto wa Mbu to Ngorongoro Crater via the conservation area. You will stop at the crater rim to enjoy the view of the drive down heading towards the crater floor for the half-day game drive. undoubtedly, Ngorongoro is one of the only places you see the rare rhino, not to mention the pride of lions and the magnificent black-maned lions. You are likely to see many other animals and bird species like leopards, hippo, flamingos, antelopes and other small mammals of all kinds. In the late afternoon after lunch, you will drive back to Moshi at the lodge for dinner and an overnight stay.

    • Day 4: Departure

      We shall be making sure you make it to the airport safe and ready for your departure on time. Until we meet again. Asante!!

  • 4 Days

    Camping Safari in Tarangire and Ngorongoro Crater

    • Day 1: Arrival/Airport pick up:

      Following your arrival at either Kilimanjaro airport or Arusha, after clearing all customs you will be picked by our professional driver, to the lodge for the briefing and all the necessary information about your trip, dinner and overnight stay

    • Day 2: Drive to Ngorongoro Crater:

      On this day you will depart from Moshi/Arusha to Ngorongoro crater passing through the Ngorongoro conservation area and after all the formalities are done you will descent to the crater floor for a game drive and have a picnic lunch at the hippo pool picnic site. After lunch, you will continue with the game drive searching for the rarely seen rhino, and as it is well known Ngorongoro is home to the black-maned lions. You are also likely to see many other animals and bird species like leopards, hippo, flamingos, antelopes and other small mammals of all kinds. Before dark, you will drive to the campsite for dinner and an overnight stay. All meals included

    • Day 3: Ngorongoro to Serengeti national park:

      After breakfast, you will depart from your lodge to the most famous national park and one among the seven wonders of the world-Serengeti. You will arrive at the Serengeti gate in the afternoon and do lunch and all the formalities at the gate then continue while having a game en-route game drive on the way. You have a high chance of seeing many animals in Serengeti including the resident animals such as lions, buffalo, elephants, cheetah, baboons, zebra with so many bird species. Overnight stay at the campsite. All meals included.

    • Day 4: Serengeti full day game drive:

      Woke up on a full day game drive, an early morning game drive within Serengeti. This is the best time to see the sunrise and also to spot the cat's family while they are actively hunting in the morning. After that you will go back to the lodges/camp for brunch and break until late afternoon where you will then go watch the sunset in the plain of Serengeti. before the dark drive back to the campsite for dinner and an overnight stay.

    • Day 5: Serengeti to Moshi/Arusha

      This will be based on your choice -an early morning game drive then you will embark headed back to Moshi/Arusha. However, if your budget allows you can definitely fly from Serengeti Seronera airstrip back to Moshi/Arusha. Dinner and overnight stay at the lodge.

    • Day 6: Departure:

      We shall be making sure you make it to the airport safe and ready for your departure on time. Until we meet again. Asante!!
