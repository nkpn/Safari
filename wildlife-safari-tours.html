Tanzania Safaris

Tanzania is a country so wealthy that it’d practically take years to document all the resources

Not only is a country proud to bear witness to the highest and largest freestanding mountain in the world but also to the rich and diverse wildlife concentrations, minerals and other resources available.If Africa’s tourism opportunities were to be summarized by one single country, that country would be Tanzania.

Book the tour

The Northern Circuit national parks are a go-to destination for the lovers of wildlife, considering their rich variety of animals. However, some of these parks, such as Tarangire National Park, are seasonal. Lake Manyara National Park, Tarangire National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, Serengeti National Park, Lake Natron, and Lake Eyasi are some examples of the Northern Circuit destinations. Morning and evening game drives and walking tours are popular activities in Tarangire, as are night game drives (though not all accommodations offer this type of tour), bird watching, and Tarangire balloon safaris in private reserves and concessions.

We’ve different packages and can definitely arrange the safari according to your preferences in different parks in the northern circuit. Whether it is Camping safari, mid-range lodge safari or budget safari, luxury safari and super luxury safari just feel free to let us know what you are looking for and our professional sales consultant will be at your service with a tailored safari package.

The good news is we also have the bush to beach package(Safari and Zanzibar)

  • 2 Days

    Camping Safari in Tarangire and Ngorongoro Crater

    • Day 1: Arrival, Airport pick up

      You will be picked by our professional driver, to the lodge for the briefing and all the necessary information about your trip, dinner and overnight stay.

    • Day 2: Moshi, Arusha to Tarangire

      Your day will begin with an early morning breakfast after your safari will commence from Moshi/Arusha heading to Tarangire national park. Like it said, Tarangire is home to a multitude of migratory wildlife species which can be found in the permanent waters of the Tarangire River. You’ll enjoy the half-day game drive and have a picnic lunch in the park. In the later afternoon, you will have to drive to the campsite in Mto wa Mbu for dinner and an overnight stay.

    • Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater to Moshi

      On this day you will have a very early morning breakfast then drive from Mto wa Mbu to Ngorongoro Crater via the conservation area. You will stop at the crater rim to enjoy the view of the drive down heading towards the crater floor for the half-day game drive. undoubtedly, Ngorongoro is one of the only places you see the rare rhino, not to mention the pride of lions and the magnificent black-maned lions. You are likely to see many other animals and bird species like leopards, hippo, flamingos, antelopes and other small mammals of all kinds. In the late afternoon after lunch, you will drive back to Moshi at the lodge for dinner and an overnight stay.

    • Day 4: Departure

      We shall be making sure you make it to the airport safe and ready for your departure on time. Until we meet again. Asante!!

    Camping Safari in Tarangire and Ngorongoro Crater

    • Day 1: Arrival/Airport pick up

      Flilowing your arrival at either Kilimanjaro airport or Arusha, after clearing all customs you will be picked by our professional driver, to the lodge for the briefing and all the necessary information about your trip, dinner and overnight stay

    • Day 2: Drive to Ngorongoro Crater

      On this day you will depart from Moshi/Arusha to Ngorongoro crater passing through the Ngorongoro conservation area and after all the formalities are done you will descent to the crater floor for a game drive and have a picnic lunch at the hippo poli picnic site. After lunch, you will continue with the game drive searching for the rarely seen rhino, and as it is well known Ngorongoro is home to the black-maned lions. You are also likely to see many other animals and bird species like leopards, hippo, flamingos, antelopes and other small mammals of all kinds. Before dark, you will drive to the campsite for dinner and an overnight stay. All meals included

    • Day 3: Ngorongoro to Serengeti national park

      After breakfast, you will depart from your lodge to the most famous national park and one among the seven wonders of the world-Serengeti. You will arrive at the Serengeti gate in the afternoon and do lunch and all the formalities at the gate then continue while having a game en-route game drive on the way. You have a high chance of seeing many animals in Serengeti including the resident animals such as lions, buffalo, elephants, cheetah, baboons, zebra with so many bird species. Overnight stay at the campsite. All meals included.

    • Day 4: Serengeti full day game drive

      Woke up on a full day game drive, an early morning game drive within Serengeti. This is the best time to see the sunrise and also to spot the cat's family while they are actively hunting in the morning. After that you will go back to the lodges/camp for brunch and break until late afternoon where you will then go watch the sunset in the plain of Serengeti. before the dark drive back to the campsite for dinner and an overnight stay.

    • Day 5: Serengeti to Moshi/Arusha

      This will be based on your choice -an early morning game drive then you will embark headed back to Moshi/Arusha. However, if your budget allows you can definitely fly from Serengeti Seronera airstrip back to Moshi/Arusha. Dinner and overnight stay at the lodge.

    • Day 6: Departure:

      We shall be making sure you make it to the airport safe and ready for your departure on time. Until we meet again. Asante!!

The Wilderness and Cultural experience safari

This particular safari invlives camping at Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Ngorongoro and Serengeti.

Available in group too →
  • 3 Days

    Tarangire National park, Ngorongoro Crater and Maasai Steppe

    • Day 1: Arrival/Airport pick up

      Flilowing your arrival at either Kilimanjaro airport or Arusha, after clearing all customs you will be picked by our professional driver, to the lodge for the briefing and all the necessary information about your trip, dinner and overnight stay.

    • Day 2: Drive to Tarangire overnight at Karatu

      Your day will begin with an early morning breakfast after your safari will commence from Moshi/Arusha heading to Tarangire national park. Like it said, Tarangire is home to a multitude of migratory wildlife species which can be found in the permanent waters of the Tarangire River. You’ll enjoy the half-day game drive and have a picnic lunch in the park. In the later afternoon, you will have to drive to the lodge in Karatu for dinner and an overnight stay.

    • Day 3: Karatu to Ngorongoro crater via conservation area overnight at the crater rim

      On this day it is best to leave the hotel as early as possible so you can see the variety of animals that are only active during the morning hours; breakfast will be packed in lunch boxes specifically for this purpose. At 6:30 AM you will set off for Ngorongoro Conservation Area, and after about an hour you will already be inside the crater. Ngorongoro is one of the most famous and interesting of Tanzania’s national parks because here you can find the greatest number of different animal species per square kilometer. You will have the chance to see buffalo, elephants, lions, wildebeests, zebras, hippopotamus and feline species such as caracals and servals. Before dark you will drive to the lodge for dinner and overnight stay

    • Day 4: Maasai Steppe to Moshi/Arusha

      Maasai is one of East Africa's most emblematic tribes. They are semi-nomadic people located primarily in Kenya and Northern Tanzania they are considered to be part of the Nilotic family of African tribal groups. You will have a chance to visit Maasai Boma in the Ngorongoro conservation area to experience their history, traditions and culture , sing and dance along with them. Before late afternoon you drive back to Moshi/Arusha at the lodge for dinner and overnight stay.

    • Day 5: Departure

      We shall be making sure you make it to the airport safe and ready for your departure on time. Until we meet again. Asante!!
